# Connect

A QR-code networking card. Type where you met someone and what you spoke about; show the QR; they scan it and their mail app opens a pre-filled email to you, carrying the context so your reply is grounded.

Pure client-side, no backend. Live at https://mw572.github.io/connect/

Edit your details (email, name, LinkedIn, brief) in the app under "Your details" — saved per device.
